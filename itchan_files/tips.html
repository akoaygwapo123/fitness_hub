Health & Fitness Tips

Stay Hydrated

Proper hydration is essential for peak performance and recovery.

The 80/20 Rule

Eat clean, whole foods (rice, chicken, eggs, veggies) 80% of the time. Allow yourself treats 20% of the time so you don't burn out.

Protein is King

Aim for a portion of protein in every meal to help your muscles recover after workouts

Focus

Don't get distracted. Focus on your set, rest for 2-3 minutes every set, and go again.

Progressive Overload

Try to do a little more each week—one more rep, 1kg more weight, or better form. If it doesn't challenge you, it won't change you.

Mind-Muscle Connection

Don't just move the weight. Feel the muscle you are targeting (like your chest or biceps) doing the work.

Warm up is Mandatory

Spend 5 minutes doing dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings) to tell your body it’s time to perform.
